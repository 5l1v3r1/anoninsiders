May started with a bang and a festive air full of worldwide solidarity and maypoles that even law enforcement could not squelch. And without further ado, this week on planet Anonymous:

#Mayday

This year May Day was embraced by Anonymous in solidarity with the countless worker groups. #Mayday made the top ten trends on twitter, no small feat! But of course, it is really in the streets  that the real action took place and it was EPIC!!! It was truly amazing by its breath and unbelievable by its diversity: a case in point for “United as One,Divided by Zero”.  Of course, the copz and general apparatus of repression tried metaphorically to rain on our parades, be it in Istanbul or Seattle, but you cant contain fireworks once the wick is lit :). And of course, there are the usual suspects trying to co-opt any successful grassroots movement… Thanks Mr. Obama, but noes…
