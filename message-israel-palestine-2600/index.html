Greetings to the state of Israel, we are anonymous.

And ultimately, Palestine, has reached their point of screaming justice to the face of evil.

Instead of organizing an investigation that would lead to the perpetrators of this heinous act that lead to the death of the 3 Israeli teens, Israel, insisted an all out collective punishment attack against Palestine.
