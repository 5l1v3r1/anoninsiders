The internet operates without a central governing body

A frontier of opportunity

Ways to set up functional activities, and organizing projects.

Why must there be a law a bill on the Internet.
A place that holds data, history, and information?

What are these governments trying to prove?
What are they trying to hide?

The Internet is too big, too important to be entrusted to these governments.

These governments that lie, that side with businesses and neglect the people. These governments that wish to control freedom and censor dissent. These are not the people to be trusted with the gift of the Internet.
