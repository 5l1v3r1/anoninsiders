That dying system, with its no sense projects, is taking us with him in its fall, and doesn’t hesitate, as recently, to go far enough to kill a person : Rémi Fraisse.

We fight for:

#MilleVaches – Dairy industries having more than 1000 cows.
#GCO – Building of a highway around Strasbourg, an economic and ecologic waste.
