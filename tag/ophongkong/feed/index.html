Our crime is to take action to the best of our abilities in the name of freedom, to give a voice to all the Hong Kong protesters who want a democracy. Try to stop us.

“The Chinese government says that it is ready for any attempt by Anonymous to illegally access its documents or tamper with its websites.”
Really? In response to the Beijing de rigueur reassurance that all is well under the sun, we are confirming that as we are typing this message, we are extracting data from the Chinese Government databases. They have already failed. We have already won.

Now, where do you come in dear readers? On October 11th at 1PM MST we will begin our DDoS attacks on every Chinese Government servers. We will be releasing all the information from the 50+ databases we have already infiltrated. We promise you a heck of a show and the price of the ticket is for you to get on your twitter and let it snow! let it snow! let it snow! all over the internet. Be part of it all and join in solidarity with the citizens of Hong Kong. Below is the link to our twitter package.
