Hacktivists being treated like terrorists?

The internet calls it hacktivism, the US government terrorism.

People like Barrett Brown, Jeremy Hammond and many more are facing an unjust decision.

http://freebarrettbrown.org/ has released on 20.02.2013 what Barrett Brown is facing:

*******************************************************************************************
Indictment #1: YouTube/Twitter, 3 counts: TOTAL 20 YEARS

Internet Threats, 18 U.S.C. § 875(c): 5 YEARS
Conspiracy to Make Publicly Available Restricted Personal Information of an Employee of the United States, 18 U.S.C. § 371 (18 U.S.C. § 119): 5 YEARS
Retaliation Against a Federal Law Enforcement Officer, 18 U.S.C. §§ 115(a)(1)(B) and (b)(4): 10 YEARS

Indictment #2: Stratfor, 12 counts: TOTAL 45 YEARS

Traffic in Stolen Authentication Features, 18 U.S.C. §§ 1028(a)(2),(b)(1)(B), and (c)(3)(A); Aid and Abet, 18 U.S.C. § 2: 15 YEARS
Access Device Fraud, 18 U.S.C. §§ 1029(a)(3) and (c)(1)(A)(i); Aid and Abet, 18 U.S.C. § 2: 10 YEARS
Aggravated Identity Theft, 18 U.S.C. §§ 1028A(a)(1); Aid and Abet, 18 U.S.C. § 2: 20 YEARS (10 counts x 2 yrs/ea)

Indictment #3: Obstruction of Justice, 2 counts: TOTAL 40 YEARS

Obstruction: Concealment of Evidence, 18 U.S.C. § 1519; Aid and Abet, 18 U.S.C. § 2: 20 YEARS
Obstruction: Corruptly Concealing Evidence, 18 U.S.C. § 1512(c)(1); Aid and Abet, 18 U.S.C. § 2: 20 YEARS

TOTAL MAXIMUM SENTENCES:
105 YEARS

*******************************************************************************************
