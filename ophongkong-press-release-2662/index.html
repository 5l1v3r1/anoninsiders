From AnonAsia : #OpHongKong Press Release

Greetings, Citizens of The United Hong Kong

We are Anonymous Asia,
On the 28th of September 2014, Hong Kong police force use shields, guns, and other unresonable force to peaceful protestors striking for democracy and universal suffrage.

Anonymous Asia will be participating in the global Anonymous operation,
operation Hong Kong. Hong Kong and Asia Anonymous members will provide full support to the operation. Hong Kong government, your power means nothing here, this is the internet.

Hong Kong citizens, you are not alone in this fight, anonymous members all over the world stand with you. We are watching, and take action to any violence used at the citizens.
It’s time for Hong Kong citizens to get real democracy.

Hong Kong government you can’t kill the idea, it is bulletproof.

And it is time to gather different demands of protestors, rather than only focusing on the technical problems. We hope Hong Kong citizens stop useless arguments, and strike for your demands. Remember, there is no leaders in this movement.

Take your heart and take your streets, we will support you in every possible way. Hong Kong citizens, keep calm and carry on. United as one, Divided by zero.
