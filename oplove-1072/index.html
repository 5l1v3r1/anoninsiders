#OpLove – United by the things we all enjoy

Alternative video

If you were to turn on the television and watch the news it wouldnt seem as if there is much love in the world
If you were to open a magazine and see an ad for a product it would seem that only a certain group can be loved
If you were to see a movie you would be given an image of what love is supposed to be

So what is love to you?

The news will not tell you about the abundance of love in the world
The magazines do not promote universal love
We know it exists, yet we are told to focus on the hate in the world that seperates us
When all of our attention should be given to those things which unite us

Enjoying a beautiful sunny day
Sharing a meal with loved ones
Seeing the smile on a face of a child
There are so many things of this world to be loved that we love
That we prevent from sharing with others because of fear
Fear is the weapon being weilded by the machine

So WE THE PEOPLE must strike a fatal blow with love

Therefore OPERATION LOVE is being initiated
This is a two phase operation to maximize success
The first phase of OPERATION LOVE is bringing awareness to the masses through virtual communication
No matter what it is that you love it is love and needs to be shared with all

Do you love animals?
Do you love children?
Do you love art?
Do you love spirituality?
Do you love politics?
Do you love love?

No matter what it is that you love it is love and needs to be shared with all
When you do, use hashtag #OpLOVE to expand the awareness of your love

The second phase of OPERATION LOVE is bringing awareness to the masses through physical activity

This is the most difficult of the operation because it will force you to deal with the threshold of your comfort zone
This is also the most rewarding part of the operation because it allows you to be creative in how you express love to and for others

Are you going to feed the hungry?
Are you going to give hugs to strangers?
Are you going to protest and profess your love in a public forum?
However you choose to share your love with the world do it wholeheartedly and do not be discouraged to do so

OPERATION LOVE is for all of the people of the world to participate in

You do not need to be a computer hacker
You do not need to wear a Guy Fawkes mask
All you need is love!

Love for your fellow human being enough to show them
There is no greater force in the world than love
There is no greater power in the world than love
Therefore no threat is even that when love is strongly present

We are ALL Anonymous
We do not forgive intollerance
We do not forget insensitivity
Do Not Expect Us
WE ARE HERE!

OPERATION LOVE ENGAGED
